MUN

MUN Conference JECRC MUN JECRC MUN

JECRC Model United Nations (MUN) in Jaipur, Rajasthan, is a premier platform uniting intellect with diplomacy. Established in 2012, it's the region's largest MUN, fostering the leaders of tomorrow through open dialogue on complex global issues. JECRC MUN inspires, challenges, and equips youth for regional and national progress. It's more than just a conference; it's an enriching experience that shapes perspectives and enhances personal growth. Participants attest to its impact on their intellect and character. Beyond committee rooms, it's a celebration of ideas and collaboration. Join us in creating a space where students refine skills and contribute to a better, interconnected world.
